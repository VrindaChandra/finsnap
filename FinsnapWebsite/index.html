Go Finance, Go Digital, Go Girl!

Finsnap™ is a one stop shop for personal finance education and digital tips for girls to become financially mindful and digitally smart.

What We Do!

We aim to demystify the world of personal finance via our website, subscriber based newsletter and app. We also host webinars for schools, colleges and individual gatherings!

Important Milestones!

We have been featured in the Economic Times and Delhi Times newspapers, CNBC Awaaz on national television and on the radio channel 93.5 RedFm!



WHAT WE DO


For modern teenagers, sources of information are tremendous, options are plenty and confusion tops everything else. With so many avenues available, one tends to overlook the basics and our priorities become ease over quality. Finsnap is an effort to demystify finance and make it fun while spreading financial awareness among females (young adults and teenagers), along with a digital guide to keep them engaged and help them optimise their expenses.
